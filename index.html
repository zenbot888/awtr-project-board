Event design department

Human Connection Design Map

Translate the neuroscience into day-of moments. People need repeated signals: I am safe here. I am welcome here. I am part of this.

Science-backed rule

Community is biological infrastructure.

  • Social connection activates reward systems.
  • Trusted people reduce perceived effort and stress.
  • Shared rhythm — breath, music, movement — bonds groups.
Design standard

Connection moments every 15–30 minutes.

Warm greeting, name/prompt tags, partner reflection, small-circle share, group breath, music, movement, tea, closing reflection.
