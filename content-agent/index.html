Storytelling formula

1. Hook
One strong line. Stop the scroll.
2. Tension
Name the problem or feeling.
3. Turn
Your insight / reframe.
4. Proof
Scene, receipt, story, photo, event.
5. Meaning
Why it matters to the person reading.
6. CTA
Reply, DM, link, save, come.
