# Awaken The Rhythm Within — Project Board

This is the live working project board for AWTR 2026, updated by Maya/Hermes from Paris’s notes.

## Strategic Shift From Sacred Village Research

Awaken should not be positioned primarily as a breathwork festival.

Positioning direction:
> A playground for humans who miss feeling alive.

Primary outcomes to design for:
- Belonging
- Community
- Play
- Aliveness
- Nature
- Music
- Human connection
- Wellness exploration

Core design principle:
People may come for wellness, but they will remember the moments between the workshops.

Success metric:
- “I felt alive.”
- “I met amazing people.”
- “I want to come back.”
- “I remembered how to play.”
